## Repo role

This repo owns:

- advisory proposal simulation orchestration
- advisory workspace drafting and handoff
- proposal lifecycle persistence, approvals, consent, and execution readiness
- backend-owned proposal decision summary and proposal alternatives

This repo does not own:

- discretionary management workflows
- canonical portfolio source data
- risk methodology authority
- performance analytics authority
